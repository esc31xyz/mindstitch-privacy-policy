# Privacy Policy for Mindstitch

**Last Updated: March 13, 2026**

Mindstitch is a local-first application designed to give you total control over your personal data. This policy outlines exactly how your information is handled within the app's current feature set.

## 1. Local-Only Architecture & Optional Sync
Mindstitch is built as a standalone utility. **Your personal data is never transmitted to our servers.**

All information remains strictly under your control:
- **Core Features:** All entries in the Dashboard, Schedule, Notes, reminders, and Finance Hub are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData.
- **iCloud Sync (Optional):** If you enable Cloud Sync (Premium), your data is encrypted and synced across your own devices using your Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to this data, nor do we host any of it on our own servers.
- **Accounts:** There are no online accounts or cloud-based profiles. Your identity is tied only to the local copy of the app and your iCloud account if sync is enabled.

## 2. Permissions and Usage
The app requests the following permissions to function:
- **Biometric Authentication (Face ID/Touch ID):** Used strictly on-device to lock your financial data and settings. We never have access to your biometric information.
- **Calendar Access:** Used to sync your Apple Calendar events into the Mindstitch Schedule and Planner for a unified view. This data is read locally and remains on your device (or your iCloud).
- **Camera & Photo Library:** Used only if you choose to select a profile picture for your local profile.
- **Notifications:** Used to deliver local alerts for your schedule and reminders. These do not require an external server.

## 3. Data Ownership & Security
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. Your usage patterns are private to you.
- **Backups:** Your data is managed by your standard device backups (e.g., iCloud or local Mac backups). We do not have access to these backups.
- **Data Deletion:** You can use the "Clear All Data" feature in Settings to instantly wipe all local records. Deleting the app will also remove all stored information.

## 4. Third-Party Integration
- **Payments:** Subscription and purchase data is handled securely by Apple via StoreKit.
- **No Financial APIs:** Mindstitch does not connect to external bank APIs or financial aggregators. All transaction logging is manual or imported via CSV.

## 5. Contact
For questions regarding our privacy-first approach, please visit the [Mindstitch GitHub Repository](https://github.com/esc31xyz/mindstitch).
