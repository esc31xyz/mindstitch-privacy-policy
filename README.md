# Privacy Policy for Mindstitch
**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**
Mindstitch is a local-first application designed to give you total control over your personal data. This policy outlines exactly how your information is handled within the app's current feature set.
## 1. Local-Only Architecture
Mindstitch is built as a standalone utility. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.** 
All information remains strictly on your device:
- **Core Features:** All entries in the Dashboard, Schedule, Notes, reminders, and Finance Hub are stored locally.
- **Accounts:** There are no online accounts or cloud-based profiles. Your identity is tied only to the local copy of the app.
## 2. Permissions and Usage
The app requests only the following permissions to function:
- **Camera & Photo Library:** Used only if you choose to select a profile picture for your local profile.
- **Notifications:** Used to deliver local alerts for your schedule and reminders. These do not require an external server.
## 3. Data Ownership & Security
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. Your usage patterns are private to you.
- **Backups:** Your data is managed by your standard device backups (e.g., iCloud or local Mac backups). We do not have access to these backups.
- **Data Deletion:** You can use the "Clear All Data" feature in Settings to instantly wipe all local records. Deleting the app will also remove all stored information.
## 4. No Third-Party Integration
Mindstitch currently has no integrations with external financial APIs, cloud sync services, or social media platforms. Everything you do stays between you and your device.
